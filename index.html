Stay with us

Residences

The Saltwood is more than a hotel; it’s a haven. Our six residences are perfect for anyone looking for a regular base in London. Whether you need one, two or three bedrooms, all of these pied à terres are sumptuous, beautifully designed and completely self-contained. Inside, you’ll find a private kitchen and dining area, space to work and state-of-the-art technology. You can even add a private chef, a valet and 24-hour room service.

THE DINING ROOM AND BAR AT THE SALTWOOD

No service times, just suggestions

Dining at The Saltwood highlights our laid-back approach to luxury. On the ground floor, you'll find the cosy and contemporary Drawing Room. Comfortable armchairs, enveloping sofas and a carefully curated art collection combine to lend a club-like atmosphere. On the lower ground floor, the Dining Room offers a menu that changes with the seasons to showcase the very best produce.

Our neighbourhood

On our doorstep, you'll find one of portharcourt most iconic neighbourhoods, St James's. Famous palaces, parks, and the QE Conference Centre are all a short walk away. Stroll to Tate Britain through residential streets, wander through the endless parks, or hop on the tube to discover everything that London has to offer from our incredible location. St. James's Park Underground station is just 100m away whilst the central hub that is Victoria Station is also within close walking distance.
